Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept reservations?
Yes, we highly recommend reserving a table for evenings and weekends to guarantee your spot. You can reserve through this page, call us directly, or use our partner platforms like EazyDiner.
Is there parking available?
Limited parking is available near the premises in the HUDA Techno Enclave area. We advise using ride-sharing services or tech park shuttle options during peak hours (7-10 AM, 5-8 PM).
Is the space suitable for large groups or meetings?
Yes, our spacious layout can accommodate groups. For groups larger than 6, please call us in advance so we can prepare a suitable arrangement. We also have semi-private areas suitable for small team meetings.
Do you offer delivery?
Yes, you can order your favorite items via our delivery partners Swiggy and Zomato. Our full menu (except certain hot beverages best enjoyed fresh) is available for delivery within a 5km radius of Hitech City.
Are you pet-friendly?
While we love pets, we only allow them in our outdoor seating area. Service animals are welcome throughout the café. Please ensure pets are leashed and well-behaved.
Do you have WiFi and charging points?
Absolutely! We offer complimentary high-speed WiFi for all guests, and there are power outlets at nearly every table as well as dedicated charging stations throughout the café.
